About me / my story

I’m Otis Valen — a digital designer with a big imagination and an even bigger soft spot for color, rhythm, and character. My work sits at the intersection of fun and function, where bold visuals meet thoughtful detail.

I’ve always believed design should feel alive. It should surprise you, invite you in, and make you look twice — not just because it’s pretty, but because it has something to say. That’s why I focus on building work that connects: clean interfaces that hum with personality, brands that feel human, and interactions that feel like they’re winking at you.

When I’m not deep in Figma or chasing the perfect color palette, I’m sketching half-serious logo ideas, experimenting with type, or building little digital side quests. I like projects that challenge the rules — and clients who are up for the ride.
